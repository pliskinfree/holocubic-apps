FluidPendant

用点阵流体效果呈现时间和动态装饰。

Shows time with a dot-matrix fluid visual effect.

功能说明

Features

  • 1流体点阵点阵会随模拟流体持续变化。
  • 2时间显示画面中显示小时与分钟，适合作为桌面挂件。
  • 3离线运行本地渲染，无需网络连接。
  • 1Fluid dotsDot matrix animation evolves like a fluid display.
  • 2Clock displayShows hour and minute as a desk pendant.
  • 3Offline displayRendered locally without network access.
