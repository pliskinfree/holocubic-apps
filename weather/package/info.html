Weather

显示当前天气、时间和三日预报。

Shows current weather, time, and a three-day forecast.

功能说明

Features

  • 1实时天气通过 CubicServer 天气接口获取当前天气。
  • 2三日预报左右按键可切换到预报页面。
  • 3城市设置读取设备设置中的天气地址。
  • 1Current weatherGets current weather through the CubicServer weather API.
  • 23-day forecastUse left or right key to switch to forecast view.
  • 3Location settingsReads weather location from device settings.
